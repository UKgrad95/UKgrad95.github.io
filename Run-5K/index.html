5K Race Route through Southport, NC

This mostly flat, 5K (3.1 miles) route takes you through the heart of Southport, North Carolina. Start and Finish near Waterfront park. American Fish Company was a restaurant/bar featured in the movie 'Safe Haven' and Fishy Fishy Cafe is located just around the corner from the Southport Marina.
