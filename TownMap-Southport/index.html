Welcome to Southport, NC ... Play-Eat-Walk Local!

Created by: Sean Stewart

Southport, North Carolina is small town "beach" community located 45 minutes south of Wilmington, NC and approximately one hour north of Myrtle Beach, SC. This map shows a 1-mile walking distance from all parks to all nearby places to eat and drink. As you can see most are along the North Howe Street corridor. Southport is home to one of the largest Fourth of July celebrations in the United States. The map was created by using data from OpenStreetMap contributors.
