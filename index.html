About me

Hello, I'm Sean. I'm a Geographer and Cartographer.

I'm currently the Geographic Information Systems Specialist/Installation Geospatial Information & Services Manager at the Military Ocean Terminal at Sunny Point, North Carolina.

I am also the Owner, Founder, Creator, CEO, and HMFIC of Ronin Geospatial.

I enjoy maps and cartography. I have an interest in mapping hiking trails, caves, 5K routes, battlefields, historic sites, and other places of interests. In my free time I still enjoy playing soccer with my Charlotte Wings Soccer Club teammates as well as jogging, hiking, surfing, shelling and shark tooth hunting.
